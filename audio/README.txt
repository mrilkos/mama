🎙️ HOW TO ADD YOUR VOICE RECORDINGS
────────────────────────────────────────
Record yourself saying each phrase, then drop the
files here with these exact names:

  ilove-darija.mp3      ←  كنبغيك يا ماما  (Kanbghik ya mama)
  ilove-arabic.mp3      ←  أحبكِ يا أمي  (Ouhibbouki ya ommi)
  ilove-french.mp3      ←  Je t'aime, Maman
  ilove-english.mp3     ←  I love you, Mom
  ilove-spanish.mp3     ←  Te quiero, Mamá
  ilove-italian.mp3     ←  Ti amo, Mamma
  ilove-portuguese.mp3  ←  Eu te amo, Mãe
  ilove-german.mp3      ←  Ich liebe dich, Mama
  ilove-turkish.mp3     ←  Seni seviyorum, Anne
  ilove-japanese.mp3    ←  お母さん、大好き (Okāsan, daisuki)
  ilove-korean.mp3      ←  엄마, 사랑해요 (Eomma, saranghaeyo)
  ilove-chinese.mp3     ←  妈妈，我爱你 (Māma, wǒ ài nǐ)
  ilove-russian.mp3     ←  Я люблю тебя, мама
  ilove-greek.mp3       ←  Σ'αγαπώ, Μαμά
  ilove-hindi.mp3       ←  मैं तुमसे प्यार करता हूँ, माँ
  ilove-swahili.mp3     ←  Nakupenda, Mama

Tips:
- Use your phone's Voice Memos app, export as MP3
- 3–8 seconds per clip is perfect
- Speak slowly and clearly — let her feel every word
- Cards still look beautiful before files are added;
  they show a gentle hint if a file is missing

To add/change languages, edit the TRACKS array
at the top of  js/voice.js
